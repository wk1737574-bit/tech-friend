Cyber Security: Internet Ki Deewar

Published: October 2025 | Author: Waqas

Cyber Security aaj ke digital era me bohot important hai. Har organization aur individual apni data security ke liye advanced techniques use kar rahe hain. Hackers aur malware constantly naye methods create karte hain, isliye hamesha updated rehna zaroori hai.

Basic steps like strong passwords, two-factor authentication, aur safe browsing habits follow karna bohot help karta hai. Professionals firewalls, encryption aur anti-virus tools use karte hain, taake sensitive information safe rahe.

Future me cyber attacks aur complex hone wale hain, lekin sath hi AI aur automated security systems help karenge data ko protect karne me. Knowledge aur awareness hi best weapon hai cyber threats ke against.
